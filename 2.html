The magic of improvisation

Last august I was back to a music session of improvisation. It was at Moment's Notice in London. Curated by Alice Z. & Geroge N., they put together a trio, a duo and then all musicians improvised together. After a long time without listening to live music I was captivated (once again) by the magic of an improvised session.

That same month, while wandering in a book shop it appear the book "Free Play" by Stephen Nachmanovich.

This two moments brought to my mind beautiful memories where I was ipnotized by improvisation. Since being at my first music jam session (WTF! 2017) I became addicted to be present on those moments where artists/creators meet to share ideas, to create spontaneouly, listing to each other, paying attetion to every detail or just feeling the energy of everyone involved...without talking, just flowing...

This #2 issue is a collection of some memories of those magical moments of spotaneus collective creation.

Flor S.
