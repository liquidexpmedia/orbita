Orbita

A zine by LIQUID EXPERIMENTAL MEDIA

Orbiting our collective artistic practice are proposals that create spaces for knowledge exchange.

The zine experiments with each issue's content: images and words intertwine, driven by themes or moments of discovery, aiming to spark curiosity and critical thinking about technology, art, and interdisciplinary creation.

It’s a window into what inspires us, reflecting the connections we make and the ideas that challenge and surprise us… within and beyond the realms of art.
